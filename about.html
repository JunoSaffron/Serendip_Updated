ABOUT US

We are a Knitwear manufacturer based in India. We make the styles for adults, kids and for the newborn using 100% organic cotton, 100% wool, cotton blended recycled polyester, mulesing- free merino wool and lamb’s wool. We also do acrylic and blended acrylic styles based on customer requests.

We have an exclusive sampling department, which is fully equipped with the essential infrastructure. We have a team of highly qualified professionals.

Our programmers use Shima Seiki APEX 4 (Latest version) software to develop competitive and customized designs.

Sampling lead time takes 2-4 weeks depending upon the yarn availability.

In parallel, we also focus on developing customized jersey wear for our premium customers.



What we do?

  • Circular knitting
  • Designing
  • Merchandising
  • Brushing
  • Embroidery
